What is Radmazon?

RadMazon is an organization created to ensure teenagers get everything they need.

What can you buy here?

From clothing to makeup, for anyone, RadMazon is here. You can sell your item, you can buy multiple items, and you can keep a track of your buying/selling history.

Where can you access RadMazon from?

Since this website was designed by a high-schooler in New York, at the moment this website is able to be launched ONLY from New York City residents. There is no shipping and packing charges involved...

How does RadMazon work?

If you want to buy something, you will meet up with the seller in the locations that RadMazon provides for its members. Connect with other New Yorkers and buy/sell anything today!

Need help?

No worries. Shoot us an email and we will reply in a matter of minutes.

"I was inspired by the Buying/Selling pages for many articles at school. I decided it was time that everyone can buy/sell easily."
- Radhika Kalani, founder of RadMazon.
